AURUM is introducing Neyro — a next-generation, non-custodial platform for creating and copying trading agents, designed to redefine how people trade in Web3.

Neyro combines AI-driven logic, smart-contract architecture, and fully transparent on-chain execution, giving users the ability to launch personalized trading agents or follow high-performing agents inside one structured environment while keeping full control over their funds.

At its core, Neyro is built around three key ideas:

🔹 Non-custodial by design — funds remain in the user’s own wallet, while trades are executed through smart contracts.

🔹 Creation and copying of trading agents — users can work with live agents, and the platform is built to let them create personalized agents aligned with their own strategy and risk profile.

🔹 Structured on-chain execution — every action follows a clear rules-based framework, making the process transparent, trackable, and built for disciplined execution instead of emotional decision-making.

With Neyro, AURUM is taking the next step in its evolution — moving from AI trading tools to an intelligent agent-driven execution layer where users can build, launch, copy, and scale their own on-chain trading logic.
